Why I want to visit:

Honestly, I really don’t usually watch Korea films or k-drama series, but I wanted to visit the Gyeongbokgung Palace with my girlfriend because she’s really into k-dramas. I know she’s always dreamed of traveling to South Korea, so I thought it would be special to go with her and see the place together. I also want to experience the culture and the beautiful traditional architecture in person, because seeing it on TV or online is different from actually walking around the palace and feeling the history there. Plus, it’s a chance to explore Seoul, try local food, and create memories with her while experiencing something new.

What I want to Experience

  • Walking through the palace grounds and imagining the life of kings and queens in the old times.
  • Taking pictures together in the traditional hanbok outfits and enjoying the scenery.
  • Experiencing the peaceful gardens, the changing of the guards ceremony, and just spending quality time with my girlfriend in a beautiful, historic environment.
