Why I want to visit:

My last visit to Dubai was in 2018 when I was just a kid, so I didn’t fully appreciate everything back then. Now, I really want to go back after I graduate and become financially stable. I hope that someday I’ll even have a chance to work there because the environment in Dubai is very different from the Philippines — more organized and full of opportunities. My father still works there, and visiting again would also mean seeing him more and exploring the city together. I want to see the Burj Khalifa again in person, stand in front of it with my girlfriend, try different local foods, and experience all the amazing places Dubai has to offer.

Personally, I remember feeling amazed and inspired the first time I saw the building as a kid. Now, I want to go back and feel that same excitement as an adult, noticing all the details I missed before and making memories with my girlfriend.

What I want to Experience

  • Standing in front of the Burj Khalifa and seeing its full height up close, taking photos and videos with my girlfriend.
  • Trying different Dubai foods and exploring the markets, restaurants, and cafes near downtown.
  • Visiting famous Dubai attractions, walking around the city, and enjoying the modern architecture and vibrant environment.
