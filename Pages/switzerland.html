Switzerland

✕

Why I want to visit:

Switzerland was never really on my dream list, but when my girlfriend told me about it, I started searching and I was like, “Woah, ang ganda nito!” The views are so beautiful, the mountains are everywhere since it’s one of the most mountainous countries in Europe, and Lake Thun is so clear and peaceful. That’s why I agreed with her that we should visit Switzerland someday. She always says that if we travel together, she hopes we include Switzerland, and honestly, I can already imagine how calming and refreshing it would be to be there with her.

Personally, I love nature and quiet places, so Lake Thun seems perfect for just relaxing, taking pictures, and enjoying the moment without the usual noise of the city. I also think it would be a good opportunity to experience Swiss culture, try local food, and maybe even do some boating on the lake.

What I want to Experience

  • Walking along the lake shore, feeling the cold breeze, and enjoying the crystal-clear water views.
  • Taking a boat ride with my girlfriend on Lake Thun to see the mountains from the water and take memorable pictures.
  • Exploring the small towns nearby, trying Swiss chocolates and cheese, and just enjoying the peaceful environment with my girlfriend.
