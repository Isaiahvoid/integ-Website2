Why I want to visit:

I wanted to visit this place because seeing it in movie films and pictures parang ang saya puntahan. Ever since I saw the Golden Gate Bridge on screen, I imagined what it feels like to stand there, feel the wind breeze, see the bridge up close, and look at the wide ocean. It feels like one of those places na mapapaisip ka about life while just watching the view.

Personally, I’ve always liked places near the sea because it feels calming for me. I imagine myself walking slowly on the bridge, taking photos, and just enjoying the moment without rushing. I also think it would be special to visit after achieving something big, like finishing my studies, because it would feel like a reward and a reminder that I made it far.

What I want to Experience

  • Feeling the cold wind while walking across the bridge and hearing the sound of cars passing by.
  • Seeing the ocean and maybe watching the sunset while taking quiet time to reflect.
  • Taking pictures like a tourist but also just enjoying the view without thinking too much, parang simple happiness lang.
